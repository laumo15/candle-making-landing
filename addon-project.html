Candle making

My favorite hobby... this fall

Candle making is a creative and fun hobby.

If you love candles, you can get inventive and create you own favorite scents, colors, and shapes. They're also great as gift ideas! And you'll save a lot of money as compared to store-bought candles.

Not only that! You'll also have much more control over what goes in your candles. For example, a soy-wax base is vegan, burns very well, and is clean and free of nasty chemicals.
